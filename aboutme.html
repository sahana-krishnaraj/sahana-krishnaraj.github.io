About Me

Hello! My name is Sahana Krishnaraj, and I’m a fourth-year student at the University of Virginia, majoring in Applied Statistics with a concentration in Data Science. I’m passionate about data science, especially creating engaging visualizations and exploring machine learning and computer vision.

Last summer, I interned at Kearney & Company in the Technology Group, where I analyzed public datasets to create actionable Tableau and Power BI dashboards, developed and presented an enterprise-wide AI Training Course for employees, and participated in a mock consulting project on SSA strategy leveraging Python-based machine learning models. This experience strengthened my skills in analytics, visualization, and communicating insights effectively.

Beyond the technical world, I love films and music, and I enjoy applying my data science skills to uncover interesting patterns in these industries. I’m excited to continue building personal projects that combine my love of data with creativity and storytelling.
